About Me

Artist, New Father, and My love of Ancient Myth are just a few words used to describe me. My passion to draw has been with me since a child and has followed me as I moved from state to state. It allowed me to make new friends and express myself in ways that i never knew before. My love of the Ancient Mythos has allowed me to learn about cultures that i never knew existed. Reading these ancient stories about the Greeks, Vikings, and Egyptians allowed me to humanize these giant figures. By humanizing them it showed me that not everyone is so different and that some core values have remained the same since the beginning of time. With that said being a father is the most difficult yet endearing journey ive had to face. I thought it would've been easy. Ohh was i wrong; but even with the little struggles here and there, i wouldn't ask for it any other way.
